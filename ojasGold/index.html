What is OJAS ?

OJAS, the 7th most popular B-School festival of the country is the flagship management and cultural festival of S.P. Jain Institute of Management & Research, Mumbai. With participation from 100+ B-Schools across India, the previous edition saw massive participation with 10,000 registrations in 17 events from all the domains across 3 days. We are back with the 14th edition of OJAS and cannot wait to invite you to the grand celebration. Let us onboard the OJAS XIV bandwagon and jump right in.
