About Ojas

What is OJAS ?

Crowned as the country's 7th biggest B-school Fest, OJAS is the flagship Management festival of S P Jain Institute of Management & Research, Mumbai, with participation from 100+ B-Schools across the country. Buzz talk of OJAS took over the B-school chatter. The fest in its previous editions saw massive participation of over 9000+ students registering for 25 events from all domains across 3 days, even when it was virtual. This year it's about to get only bigger and better. From the despair of a disconnected illusory world, to coming together in a post-pandemic reality, a promise of Change and Hope marks the 14th edition of OJAS. We cannot wait to invite all of you to be a part of the frenzy. Let’s onboard the most awaited Illusion of OJAS XIV and experience the extravagant Reality in all its glory and jump right into the ILLUSIONS OF REALITY!
